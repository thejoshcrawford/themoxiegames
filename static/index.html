The Moxie Games

January 15, 2017

Partnering with the Spokane Health & Fitness Expo


We are Moxie! Moxie means to be bold and beautiful, just like the women of the Northwest! This is a chance for ladies to come together, show off their fitness and celebrate the love for CrossFit we all share. A portion of every entry will go towards the UGM Crisis Shelter for Women and Children in Spokane, WA.

Divisions

  • Rx Individual - $65
  • Masters Individual (40+) - $65
  • Novice Teams of Three - $60 per athlete
  • Masters Teams of Three (40+) - $60 per athlete
  • Register early! Late registration increases on December 20th.

Movement Standards

  • Barbell Lifts: 75lbs for Rx individual and 55lbs for masters and teams
  • Dumbbells: 20lbs for Rx individual and 15lbs for masters and teams
  • Kettle Bell Movements: 35lbs for Rx individual and 26lbs for masters and teams
  • DBall: 70lbs for Rx individual and 50lbs for masters and teams
  • Geisha Creations
